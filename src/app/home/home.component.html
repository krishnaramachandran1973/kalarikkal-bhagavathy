Sri Kalarikkal Bhagavathy

Sri Kalarikkal Bhagavathy is the main deity in the temple. A divine and soothing energy, the Mother of the Universe, is Mother to a million devotees.

People from all walks of life come to have Darshan, for grant of wishes and wisdom.

The picture depicted is her form as envisioned by the Tantri.

Daily rituals are performed as prescribed in the Vedic rituals of Indian traditions.

The temple is situated at Vettikkattiri, near Cheruthuruthi, Vallathol Nagar, Shornur, Kerala. People call the temple 'Kalari'.

Speciality

Vedic rituals are performed with at-most perfection that is unheard of in other such temples. One has to experience the splendour and grandeur of such ceremonies that elevate man to realms of spirituality.

Priests

Priests are chosen according to Deva Prashna that gives insights into the Deity's wishes.

Management

Rituals are performed under the guidance and direction of the renowned astrologer, Mr. VenuGopala Panikkar.

Tantric Rituals

Vedic scriptures prescribe innumerous rituals to be performed.

The effectiveness of these rituals depends on the suitability of the ritual and the venue of the ritual among other things.

The most important is the person performing the ritual. When all these are accurate and appropriate, the effect is immediately experienced.

Astrology

Things that look ordinary at first sight often reveal a deep cosmic truth on closer examination. This is especially true in the realm of astrology.

It is an established fact that the waxing and waning of the moon affects tides and the mental condition of people. It is also true that the planets and stars have an effect on our world and our destinies.

This fact is the basis on which astrology makes predictions. These predictions sometimes call for remedial steps in the form of divine intervention invoked by rituals.
