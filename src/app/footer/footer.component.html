ABOUT KALARIKKAL BHAGAVATHY

The deity consecrated in the sanctum is named Vettikkattiri Bhagavathy, as Maha-Lakshmi. She is mother goddess of wealth, abundance, prosperity and wisdom.

She bestows her glance onto those who call her with steadfast Bhakthi and penance.
